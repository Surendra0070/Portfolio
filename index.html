About

Surendra leads quality across FreshToHome's core e-commerce systems — order management, payments & refunds, pricing & catalog, delivery logistics, and CRM/campaigns. Rather than testing modules in isolation, he coordinates QA across the microservices they depend on, so a fix in one system doesn't quietly break another. His path here started in payment gateway and pharmacy platform testing, giving him a foundation in functional, regression, and acceptance testing that now shows up in how he structures QA at scale. He's comfortable going deep into SQL, REST APIs, and mobile app internals (Cordova, React Native) to isolate root causes, and just as comfortable building the review processes and documentation that let a growing QA team move faster without cutting corners.
